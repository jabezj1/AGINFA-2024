Set on 200 acres in the foothills of the Shawangunk Mountain Range, this secluded resort on a lake is 11 miles from Minnewaska State Park. Amenities include a restaurant and a spa, as well as a gym, indoor and outdoor pools, a sauna and a hot tub. There are also 3 hiking trails, 4 tennis courts.
